💸 Best Ways to Send Money from Saudi Arabia 2026

📅 Updated January 2026  ·  ⏱ 8 min read  ·  🏷️ Expat Life

Saudi Arabia sends over $35 billion in remittances annually, making it the world's second-largest source of outbound transfers. With so many options available — from bank branches to apps — choosing the right service can save you hundreds of riyals every year.

How to Compare Remittance Services

The total cost of a transfer has two components most people miss:

  • Transfer fee: The flat or percentage fee charged upfront
  • Exchange rate margin: The difference between the mid-market rate and what they offer you

A service with "zero fees" may still cost more due to a poor exchange rate. Always compare the total amount received at the destination, not just the fee.

Tips to Maximize Your Transfer

  • Compare before you transfer: Use our Remittance Comparison Tool to see who gives you the most
  • Transfer larger amounts less frequently: Fixed fees hurt small transfers more
  • Avoid airport/hotel kiosks: Always the worst rates
  • Use apps vs. branches: App rates are usually better than walking into a branch
  • Time your transfer: Avoid sending on weekends when bank rates are sometimes worse
