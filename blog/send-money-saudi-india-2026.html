SAR to INR — Key Facts 2026

Over 2.4 million Indians live and work in Saudi Arabia, making the SAR-to-INR corridor one of the world's largest remittance routes. Saudi Arabia is the second-largest source of remittances to India globally. Choosing the right transfer service can save thousands of rupees annually.

Wise — Best Overall for SAR to INR

Wise uses the mid-market exchange rate (the "real" rate you see on Google or XE.com) and charges a small transparent percentage fee. For Indian expats in Saudi Arabia regularly sending money home, Wise consistently delivers the most INR per riyal. The Wise app works in Saudi Arabia, accepts payments from Saudi bank accounts and debit cards, and transfers to all major Indian banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, and Kotak.

How to use Wise from Saudi Arabia

  1. Visit wise.com or download the Wise app
  2. Create account with your Iqama and Indian passport
  3. Enter SAR amount and select INR destination
  4. Add recipient's Indian bank account (IFSC + account number)
  5. Pay via Saudi bank transfer or Mada/Visa debit card
  6. INR arrives in recipient's bank within 1–2 business days

Al Rajhi Bank — Most Trusted Brand for Indians in KSA

Al Rajhi Bank has operated in Saudi Arabia since 1957 and is the most trusted name for remittances among Indian expats. While the exchange rate is typically 2–3% below the mid-market rate, Al Rajhi's reliability, Saudi-language support, and widespread branch network make it the default choice for many. Best for cash pickup by family in India.

STC Pay — Best for Speed

STC Pay can transfer SAR to INR within minutes if the recipient has a bank account. Available to STC mobile subscribers via the STC Pay app. Rates are competitive — often better than Al Rajhi but slightly below Wise. No branch visit required.

Tips to Maximise INR from Saudi Arabia

  • Compare on transfer day — exchange rates change daily
  • Avoid weekends — banks often widen margins on Fridays/Saturdays
  • Send bank-to-bank — better rates than cash pickup in most cases
  • Use new-user offers — Remitly and Wise frequently offer first-transfer promotions
  • Send larger amounts less often — flat fees eat less into larger transfers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best app to send money from Saudi Arabia to India in 2026? ▼
Wise offers the best SAR to INR exchange rate (mid-market rate) with low transparent fees. STC Pay is best for speed. Al Rajhi Bank is best for cash pickup and trust. Remitly is best for first-time users due to promotional offers.
How much can I send from Saudi Arabia to India? ▼
There is no official limit on personal remittances for legal residents of Saudi Arabia. Individual service limits apply: Wise up to ~SAR 50,000/day, Al Rajhi Bank up to SAR 100,000/transfer via app. For large amounts, bank wire via your Saudi bank account is most reliable.
Is it safe to send money via Wise from Saudi Arabia? ▼
Yes. Wise is a regulated financial institution licensed in the UK (FCA) and operates legally in Saudi Arabia. It has transferred over $100 billion globally and is used by millions of expats worldwide.
How long does it take for SAR to reach India as INR? ▼
Wise: typically 1–2 business days to Indian banks. STC Pay: often within minutes. Al Rajhi Bank: 1–3 business days. Western Union: minutes for cash pickup. Remitly: minutes to 1 day depending on payment method.
