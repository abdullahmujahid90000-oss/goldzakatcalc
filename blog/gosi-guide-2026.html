How to Calculate GOSI (Social Insurance) in Saudi Arabia 2026 — Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about GOSI contributions: the rates, the wage base, the cap, and how to calculate the deduction from your salary.

Quick answer: the GOSI rate for a Saudi employee = 9.75% of (basic salary + housing allowance), capped at SAR 45,000/month. Use the free GOSI Calculator to get your result instantly.

What is GOSI (Social Insurance)?

The General Organization for Social Insurance (GOSI) is the government body responsible for providing insurance coverage to private-sector employees in Saudi Arabia. Coverage includes: pensions/retirement, the unemployment insurance scheme (SANED), and occupational hazards.

What counts toward the GOSI wage base?

  • Basic salary — always included
  • Housing allowance — included in the base
  • Transport allowance — excluded from the base
  • Phone, food and other allowances — generally excluded
⚠️ Common mistake: many employees calculate GOSI on basic salary only. The correct base is basic salary + housing allowance together (up to SAR 45,000).

Step-by-step worked example

A Saudi employee with a basic salary of SAR 10,000 + housing allowance SAR 3,000 + transport allowance SAR 1,000:

Step 1 — Determine the base: 10,000 + 3,000 = SAR 13,000 (transport is excluded)

Step 2 — Check the cap: 13,000 is below 45,000 ✅

Step 3 — Employee deduction: 13,000 × 9.75% = SAR 1,267.50

Step 4 — Employer contribution: 13,000 × 11.75% = SAR 1,527.50

What is SANED?

SANED is Saudi Arabia's unemployment insurance scheme, for Saudi employees only. Both the employee and the employer contribute 0.75% of the wage base. If a worker loses their job involuntarily, an eligible employee receives monthly payments for up to 12 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GOSI contribution rate for a Saudi employee in 2026? ▼
The Saudi employee deduction is 9.75% of the wage base (9% pensions + 0.75% SANED). The employer pays an additional 11.75%. The maximum wage base is SAR 45,000 per month.
Do expats pay GOSI in Saudi Arabia? ▼
No. Expat employees are exempt from the GOSI contribution. However, the employer pays 2% for occupational hazards on behalf of the expat employee.
Is the transport allowance included in the GOSI base? ▼
No. The transport allowance is excluded from the GOSI wage base. The base is calculated from basic salary and housing allowance only, capped at SAR 45,000.
