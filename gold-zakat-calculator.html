Frequently Asked Questions

Does Zakat apply to gold jewellery?▾
Most scholars agree Zakat applies to all gold above the Nisab threshold held for one lunar year, including jewellery. Some scholars exempt jewellery worn regularly.
What is the Nisab for gold 2026?▾
The Nisab is 85 grams of 24K gold. At ~SAR 245/g, this equals approximately SAR 20,825. Verify with current prices.
When to pay Zakat on gold?▾
Zakat is due once gold has been in your possession for one full Islamic lunar year (Haul) and exceeds Nisab. Most Muslims pay during Ramadan.
