Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Nisab for gold Zakat in 2026? ▼
The Nisab is 85 grams of pure 24K gold. At May 2026 prices (~SAR 320/gram for 24K), this equals approximately SAR 27,200. If your total gold value exceeds this threshold and you've held it for one Hijri year, Zakat is due.
How is Zakat on gold calculated? ▼
Multiply your gold weight by the current market price per gram to get the total value, then calculate 2.5% of that value. The Nisab threshold must be met and the gold held for one full Hijri year (Haul).
Does Zakat apply to gold jewellery? ▼
There is scholarly difference of opinion. The Hanafi school requires Zakat on all gold including jewellery. The Hanbali school (followed in Saudi Arabia) generally exempts jewellery for personal use. For a specific fatwa, consult a scholar or alifta.gov.sa.
