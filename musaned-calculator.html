❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Musaned contract verification fee?
The Musaned contract verification fee in Saudi Arabia is SAR 100 for standard processing or SAR 200 for urgent processing as of 2026.
Is medical test required for Musaned?
Yes, a valid medical test is mandatory for all Musaned contract verifications, costing SAR 300–500 depending on the service center.
How long does Musaned processing take?
Standard Musaned processing takes 1–3 business days. Urgent processing is completed within 24 hours for an additional fee.
Where are Musaned service centers located?
Musaned service centers are located in major cities including Riyadh (Olaya), Jeddah (Tahlia), Dammam (King Fahd Road), and other governorates.
What documents are needed for Musaned?
Required documents include: valid passport, work visa, employment contract, and medical test clearance.
Can I book Musaned appointment online?
Yes, you can book Musaned appointments through the official Musaned portal or Absher platform.
What is the difference between Musaned and Qiwa?
Musaned handles foreign worker contracts and attestations, while Qiwa manages Saudi labor law compliance and employee services.
Do domestic workers need Musaned?
Yes, all foreign domestic workers must complete Musaned contract verification before entering Saudi Arabia.
