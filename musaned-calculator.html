About Musaned Platform

Musaned (مساند) is the official Saudi government platform for recruiting and managing domestic workers. All domestic worker recruitment must go through Musaned or licensed agencies.

Minimum wages vary by nationality and are set by bilateral agreements between KSA and the worker's home country.

Employer responsibilities: Provide accommodation, meals, healthcare, and annual ticket home. The domestic worker is exempt from the expat dependent levy.
