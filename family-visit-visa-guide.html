Salary Requirement

Most professions require a minimum basic salary of SAR 3,500 to SAR 5,000. Some senior professions may require higher salaries to sponsor parents.

📋 Attested Documents

Marriage and Birth certificates must be attested by the Saudi Embassy in your home country and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Saudi Arabia.

🏢 Chamber of Commerce

You must be a member of the Chamber of Commerce. The visit request must be submitted and approved through the Chamber's online system before the visa is issued.

Important: Ensure your Iqama is valid for at least 6 months and your profession allows family sponsorship before applying.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Family Visit Visas

How much is a Family Visit Visa fee in Saudi Arabia?
The official fee for a Single Entry Family Visit Visa (30 days) is SAR 2,000. For a Multiple Entry Visa (1 year), the fee is SAR 3,000. Insurance costs are additional, typically around SAR 140 per person.
What documents are needed for a Family Visit Visa?
Required documents include: Sponsor's valid Iqama, Salary certificate (minimum SAR 3,500 - 5,000 depending on profession), Marriage/Birth certificates (attested), Passport copies, and relationship proof.
Can I bring my parents on a Family Visit Visa?
Yes, residents can sponsor parents for visit visas. The sponsor must usually have a valid profession and meet salary requirements. Some professions may require higher salaries to sponsor parents.
Is health insurance mandatory for Family Visit Visas?
Yes, health insurance is mandatory for all visit visa applicants. The cost varies based on age and duration, averaging SAR 140 for a standard 30-day policy.
How long does it take to get a Family Visit Visa approved?
Approval times vary from 24 hours to 1 week depending on the Chamber of Commerce verification and Ministry of Foreign Affairs processing. Ensure all documents are attested to avoid delays.
What is the salary requirement for Family Visit Visa?
Generally, a minimum basic salary of SAR 3,500 is required for some professions, while others require SAR 5,000 or more. It is best to check with your company PRO for your specific profession's requirement.
Can I extend a Family Visit Visa?
Single entry visas can often be extended for a fee (usually SAR 100 per extension) if the visitor has not stayed the maximum allowed period. Extensions are done via the Absher portal.
Who can apply for a Family Visit Visa?
Expatriate residents with a valid Iqama can apply for spouses, children, parents, and sometimes siblings. The sponsor must meet profession and salary criteria set by the Ministry.
