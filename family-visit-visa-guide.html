👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Visit Visa Guide Saudi Arabia 2026

Complete step-by-step guide: SAR 300 fee, SAR 500 insurance, 90-day validity, Absher application, document checklist

Updated June 2026 • Fee: SAR 300 • Insurance: SAR 500 • Validity: 90 days
In Saudi Arabia 2026, a family visit visa costs SAR 300 plus SAR 500 mandatory health insurance (total SAR 800 per visitor). It's valid for 90 days from entry and can be extended once for another 90 days. Apply through Absher with valid Iqama and relationship proof.

📖 Complete Family Visit Visa Guide

This comprehensive guide walks you through every step of obtaining a family visit visa in Saudi Arabia for 2026. Whether you're inviting your spouse, children, parents, or siblings, this guide covers all requirements, fees, processing times, and common pitfalls to avoid.

1. Eligibility Requirements

To apply for a family visit visa, you must:

  • Have a valid Iqama (residency permit)
  • Be employed in the private or public sector
  • Have no outstanding traffic or legal violations
  • Be inviting immediate family members only (spouse, children, parents, siblings)

2. Required Documents

Prepare these documents before starting your application:

  • Your valid Iqama (must be valid for at least 3 months)
  • Passport copy of visitor (valid for at least 6 months)
  • Relationship proof:
    • Spouse: Marriage certificate (attested)
    • Children: Birth certificate showing your name (attested)
    • Parents: Your birth certificate showing their names (attested)
    • Siblings: Birth certificates showing shared parents (attested)
  • Completed application through Absher portal

3. Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Log in to Absher
    Go to absher.sa and log in with your credentials.
  2. Navigate to Services
    Click on "My Services" → "Passports" → "Visa Services" → "Family Visit Visa".
  3. Fill Visitor Details
    Enter the visitor's full name, passport number, nationality, and relationship to you.
  4. Upload Documents
    Upload scanned copies of required documents (passport, relationship proof).
  5. Pay Fees
    Pay SAR 300 visa fee + SAR 500 health insurance through SADAD.
  6. Submit Application
    Review all information and submit your application.
  7. Track Status
    Check your application status under "Inquiries" → "Visa Status".

4. Fees Breakdown

Fee Type Amount (SAR) Description
Visa Fee 300 Government fee per visitor
Health Insurance 500 Mandatory coverage for 90 days
Total Cost 800 Per visitor for 90 days

5. Processing Time & Validity

  • Processing Time: 1–3 business days
  • Visa Validity: 90 days from date of issue
  • Stay Duration: 90 days from date of entry into KSA
  • Extension: Can be extended once for additional 90 days (same fees apply)

6. Common Reasons for Rejection

Avoid these common mistakes that lead to visa rejection:

  • Expired or invalid Iqama
  • Incomplete or unattested relationship documents
  • Visitor's passport expires within 6 months
  • Previous visa violations by the visitor
  • Incorrect information in the application

7. After Visa Approval

Once approved:

  1. Download and print the visa from Absher
  2. Send it to your family member
  3. They must enter Saudi Arabia within the visa validity period
  4. No medical test is required for visitors
  5. Ensure they carry the printed visa and original passport when traveling

📊 Worked Example: Family Visit Visa Application

Scenario: Engineer Inviting Parents to Riyadh

Applicant: Ali, Pakistani engineer working in Riyadh with valid Iqama until December 2026

Visitors: Father and mother (both Pakistani citizens)

Documents Prepared:

Application Process:

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the family visit visa fee in Saudi Arabia?
The family visit visa fee in Saudi Arabia is SAR 300 per visa, plus SAR 500 for mandatory health insurance, totaling SAR 800 per visitor.
How long is a family visit visa valid?
A family visit visa is initially valid for 90 days (3 months) from the date of entry into Saudi Arabia. It can be extended once for an additional 90 days.
Who can apply for a family visit visa?
Any expatriate resident with a valid Iqama can apply for family visit visas for immediate family members: spouse, children, parents, and siblings.
What documents are required?
Required documents include: valid Iqama, passport copy of visitor, relationship proof (marriage/birth certificate), and completed application form through Absher.
How long does processing take?
Family visit visa processing typically takes 1–3 business days if all documents are complete and submitted through Absher.
Is medical test required for visitors?
No, visitors on family visit visas are not required to undergo medical tests. However, they must have valid health insurance.
Can I extend a family visit visa?
Yes, you can extend a family visit visa once for an additional 90 days by paying the same fees (SAR 300 + SAR 500 insurance).
What's the difference between visit and residence visa?
Family visit visa is temporary (90 days, extendable once), while family residence visa is long-term (up to 2 years, renewable) and requires dependent levy payments.
Can parents get a family visit visa?
Yes, parents can get family visit visas. You'll need to provide proof of relationship (birth certificate showing your name).
What are common reasons for rejection?
Common rejection reasons include: incomplete documents, expired Iqama, incorrect relationship proof, or previous visa violations by the applicant.
