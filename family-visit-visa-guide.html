Understanding Family Visit Visas in Saudi Arabia (2026 Complete Guide)

Expatriate residents in Saudi Arabia with valid Iqamas can sponsor family members (spouse, children, parents, or siblings) for temporary visits through the Family Visit Visa system. This visa allows relatives to enter the Kingdom legally for holidays, weddings, medical treatment, or extended family stays. Our free calculator helps you estimate exact visa costs, mandatory insurance premiums, and total sponsorship expenses using official Absher and Ministry of Foreign Affairs rates for 2026.

💰 Current Visit Visa Fees (Official Absher 2026)

The Saudi government offers two primary family visit visa types:

  • Single Entry Visit Visa: SAR 2,000 — Valid for 30 days from date of entry. Ideal for short holidays or specific events. Can often be extended for an additional fee.
  • Multiple Entry Visit Visa: SAR 3,000 — Valid for 1 year with unlimited entries/exits within the validity period. Best for frequent visitors or long-term family stays.

Important: Health insurance is mandatory for all applicants. Standard coverage costs approximately SAR 140 per person for a 30-day policy, but varies by age, nationality, and coverage duration.

📋 Key Eligibility & Requirements

  • Sponsor Iqama Validity: Must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Minimum Salary: SAR 3,500–5,000 basic (varies by profession)
  • Chamber Membership: Must be registered with the Council of Saudi Chambers
  • Attested Documents: Marriage/birth certificates attested by Saudi Embassy & MOFA
  • Professional Restrictions: Some professions restrict sponsoring parents or extended family

⚠️ Overstay Penalties: SAR 1,000 for the first 3 days, then SAR 1,000 per day thereafter. Overstaying can result in deportation bans and future visa rejections.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Family Visit Visas

How much is a Family Visit Visa fee in Saudi Arabia?+

Single Entry: SAR 2,000 (30 days). Multiple Entry: SAR 3,000 (1 year). Insurance is additional (~SAR 140/person).

Can I bring my parents on a Family Visit Visa?+

Yes, if your profession allows and you meet salary requirements. Some senior roles may require higher minimum salaries.

Is health insurance mandatory?+

Yes, mandatory for all applicants. Cost varies by age/duration, averaging SAR 140 for standard 30-day coverage.

How long does approval take?+

Typically 24 hours to 1 week depending on Chamber verification & MOFA processing. Attested documents prevent delays.
