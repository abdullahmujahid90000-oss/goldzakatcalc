Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Iqama renewal cost in 2026? ▼
The standard Iqama renewal fee is SAR 650 per year for all nationalities. Late renewal incurs an additional SAR 500 per month penalty. If your Iqama is 3 months overdue, you pay SAR 650 + SAR 1,500 = SAR 2,150 total.
How do I renew my Iqama in Saudi Arabia? ▼
Renew via the Absher app or website (absher.sa). Login with your Iqama number or National Access Number, pay SAR 650 via SADAD or bank card, and receive instant confirmation. See our full Iqama renewal guide.
Can I travel with an expired Iqama? ▼
No. You cannot get an exit visa if your Iqama has expired. Renew first before any international travel.
