📖 Understanding Gold Prices in Saudi Arabia

Gold prices in Saudi Arabia fluctuate daily based on global market trends, currency exchange rates, and local demand. The prices listed above are for raw gold only — when purchasing jewelry, you'll pay additional making charges (maksariya) that vary by store.

Key Facts About Gold Karats

  • 24 Karat (99.9% pure): Best for investment (bars, coins). Minimal making charges.
  • 22 Karat (91.7% pure): Most common for traditional Saudi jewelry. Higher making charges.
  • 21 Karat (87.5% pure): Popular for bridal sets and heavy jewelry.
  • 18 Karat (75% pure): Used for modern, lightweight designs. More durable than higher karats.

Where to Buy Gold in Saudi Arabia

Major gold souqs include:

  • Riyadh: Deira Gold Souq, Olaya Street
  • Jeddah: Al-Balad Historic District, Tahlia Street
  • Dammam: King Fahd Road, Dhahran Mall area

You can also buy investment-grade gold from banks like Al Rajhi Bank and SNB.
