Frequently Asked Questions

What is the overtime rate in Saudi Arabia?▾
Saudi Labour Law Article 107: overtime is 150% (1.5×) of basic hourly rate. Max 3 extra hours per day.
How many hours/day in Ramadan?▾
Muslim employees work maximum 6 hours/day during Ramadan. Non-Muslims work normal hours unless the employer decides otherwise.
What is the standard working week?▾
48 hours per week (8 hours/day × 6 days) or 40 hours for a 5-day week.
