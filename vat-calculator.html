Frequently Asked Questions

What is the VAT rate in Saudi Arabia 2026? ▼
The standard VAT rate in Saudi Arabia is 15%, effective since 1 July 2020. It was raised from 5% to 15% as part of the government's fiscal response to COVID-19. Residential rent, healthcare, education, and exports are exempt or zero-rated.
How do I calculate 15% VAT in Saudi Arabia? ▼
To add VAT: multiply price by 1.15 (e.g. SAR 1,000 × 1.15 = SAR 1,150). To remove VAT from a VAT-inclusive price: divide by 1.15 (e.g. SAR 1,150 ÷ 1.15 = SAR 1,000 base).
Is food subject to VAT in Saudi Arabia? ▼
Most food items and restaurant meals are subject to 15% VAT. Some basic food staples may be zero-rated. Check with ZATCA (zatca.gov.sa) for specific rulings on individual food categories.
