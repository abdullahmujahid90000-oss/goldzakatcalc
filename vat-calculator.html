🧾 VAT Calculator Saudi Arabia 2026

Calculate 15% VAT on goods and services: add or remove tax, VAT-inclusive vs exclusive, ZATCA e-invoicing rules

Updated June 2026 • Standard Rate: 15% • ZATCA Compliant
In Saudi Arabia 2026, the standard VAT rate is 15%. To add VAT: multiply base price by 1.15. To remove VAT: divide total price by 1.15. Most goods and services are taxable, with exceptions for basic food, residential rent, and financial services.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the VAT rate in Saudi Arabia?
The standard VAT rate in Saudi Arabia is 15% as of 2026, applied to most goods and services.
How to calculate VAT in Saudi Arabia?
To add VAT: multiply base price by 1.15. To remove VAT: divide total price by 1.15.
What items are VAT exempt in Saudi Arabia?
VAT-exempt items include financial services, residential rent, local passenger transport, and certain educational services.
What is the VAT registration threshold?
Businesses must register for VAT if their taxable supplies exceed SAR 375,000 in a 12-month period.
Is VAT charged on gold in Saudi Arabia?
Yes, gold is subject to 15% VAT in Saudi Arabia as of 2026, unless exported.
What is ZATCA e-invoicing?
ZATCA e-invoicing is the mandatory electronic invoicing system for all VAT-registered businesses in Saudi Arabia.
Can tourists get VAT refund in Saudi Arabia?
Yes, tourists can claim VAT refunds on eligible purchases through the Tax Refund for Tourists Scheme.
How often do businesses file VAT returns?
VAT returns must be filed quarterly within 28 days of the end of each tax period.
Is food subject to VAT in Saudi Arabia?
Basic food items are zero-rated (0% VAT), while restaurant meals and prepared foods are subject to 15% VAT.
What is the penalty for non-compliance?
Penalties for VAT non-compliance range from SAR 1,000 to SAR 50,000 depending on the violation.
