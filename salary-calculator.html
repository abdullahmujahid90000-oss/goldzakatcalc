📖 How to Use This Saudi Salary Calculator

Our free Saudi salary calculator helps employees in Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, and across KSA understand their exact take-home pay for 2026. Whether you're negotiating a job offer, applying for a loan, or preparing a budget, this tool breaks down your salary components accurately using official GOSI rates.

  1. Enter Your Gross Salary
    This is your total monthly compensation as stated in your contract. Include all components before deductions.
  2. Select Employee Type
    Choose Saudi national or expat. For Saudi nationals, GOSI is charged on basic + housing; expat employees pay 0% GOSI.
  3. Specify Basic Salary
    For Saudi nationals, basic salary is part of the GOSI base. In Saudi contracts, basic salary is usually 40–60% of gross. Check your iqama or contract for this figure.
  4. Add Allowances
    Housing allowance is included in the GOSI base for Saudi nationals; transport is excluded. Enter these separately for accurate calculation.
  5. Calculate Net Pay
    For Saudi nationals the calculator applies 9.75% GOSI on (basic + housing), capped at SAR 45,000, and shows your final take-home amount.
  6. Use for Official Purposes
    Save or screenshot your results for salary certificates, visa applications, or rental agreements.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How is net salary calculated in Saudi Arabia?
Net salary = Gross salary − GOSI. For Saudi nationals, GOSI is 9.75% of (basic salary + housing allowance), capped at SAR 45,000. Transport and other allowances are excluded from the GOSI base. Expat employees pay 0% GOSI.
What is the GOSI deduction rate in 2026?
For Saudi employees the contribution rate is 9.75% of (basic + housing), capped at SAR 45,000 per month (max deduction SAR 4,387.50). Non-Saudi/expat employees pay 0%.
Is housing allowance included in GOSI calculation?
Yes. For Saudi nationals the GOSI contribution base is basic salary + housing allowance (capped at SAR 45,000). Transport and other allowances are excluded.
What is a salary certificate in Saudi Arabia?
A salary certificate is an official document from your employer showing gross salary, basic salary, allowances, and net pay. Required for visas, loans, and rentals.
How to get a salary certificate in KSA?
Request it from your HR department. It must be stamped and signed. Many companies provide it through Qiwa or Muqeem portals.
Does GOSI apply to expatriate employees?
No. Non-Saudi (expat) employees do not pay any GOSI from their salary (0%). Their employer pays only 2% for occupational-hazard insurance. The 9.75% employee contribution applies to Saudi nationals only.
What if my basic salary exceeds SAR 45,000?
GOSI is capped at SAR 45,000. Even if your basic salary is SAR 50,000, GOSI is calculated on SAR 45,000 only (max SAR 4,387.50).
Are there other deductions besides GOSI?
Some employers may deduct for health insurance, pension plans, or income tax (for certain nationalities), but GOSI is the only mandatory deduction for most employees.
