❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there income tax in Saudi Arabia? +
No. Saudi Arabia has no personal income tax. The only salary deduction is GOSI (9.75%) for Saudi nationals. Expats pay zero deductions.
What is the GOSI rate for 2026? +
Saudi employees contribute 9.75% of (basic + housing), capped at SAR 45,000/month. Employers add 11.75%. Expats pay 0%. Use our GOSI calculator.
How is End of Service calculated? +
Article 84: ½ month basic per year for first 5 years, then 1 full month per year. Basic salary only — excludes housing and transport. Use our EOS calculator.
What is VAT rate in Saudi Arabia 2026? +
15% since July 2020. Residential rent, healthcare, education are exempt. Use our VAT calculator.
How much does Iqama renewal cost in 2026? +
SAR 650/year. Late renewal = SAR 500/month penalty. Track with our Iqama tracker.
What is the Nisab for Zakat in 2026? +
Nisab = 85g of 24K gold ≈ SAR 27,200 at current prices. If your assets exceed this and one Hijri year has passed, Zakat (2.5%) is due. Use our Zakat calculator.
Are all calculators free? +
Yes — all 25+ tools are completely free forever. No account, no subscription. We earn from Google AdSense ads and affiliate commissions on remittance services (disclosed). This never affects our calculations.
