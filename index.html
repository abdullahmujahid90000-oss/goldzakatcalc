How to Calculate Zakat on Gold in 2026?

Zakat is a mandatory pillar of Islam. For gold, the Nisab threshold is 87.48 grams. If you own gold above this weight for a full lunar year, you must pay 2.5% of its current market value.

In Riyadh and across KSA, gold prices are currently high. Using a tool like our calculator ensures you don't overpay or underpay your religious obligation.
