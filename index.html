How it works

  • We convert your weight to grams if needed.
  • We apply purity: value = grams × price/gram × (karat/24).
  • Result shows estimated market value in your chosen currency.

Examples

Example: 10g of 22K at 270 SAR/g ≈ 10 × 270 × (22/24) = 2475 SAR.

Example: 1 tola (11.6638g) of 24K at 70 USD/g ≈ 816.47 USD.

FAQ

Use your local jeweler, bank site, or news. Enter the 24K price per gram.

Is the result exact?

It’s an estimate. Actual offers vary by dealer fees and making costs.

Can I calculate zakat?

Yes. Use the Zakat Calculator; it checks nisab and 2.5% rate.
