❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there income tax in Saudi Arabia? +
No. Saudi Arabia has no personal income tax for individuals — Saudi nationals or expats. The only salary deduction is GOSI (9.75%) for Saudi employees. Expats have zero salary deductions.
What is the GOSI rate for 2026? +
Saudi employees contribute 9.75% of (basic salary + housing allowance) to GOSI, capped at SAR 45,000/month. Employers contribute an additional 11.75%. Expats pay 0% GOSI. Use our free GOSI calculator.
How is End of Service calculated in Saudi Arabia? +
Under Saudi Labour Law Article 84: ½ month basic salary per year for the first 5 years, then 1 full month per year thereafter. Always calculated on basic salary only — not housing or transport. Use our EOS calculator.
What is the VAT rate in Saudi Arabia 2026? +
15%, effective since July 2020. Residential rent, healthcare, and education are exempt. Restaurants, retail, electronics, and commercial rent all attract 15% VAT. Use our VAT calculator.
How much does Iqama renewal cost in 2026? +
SAR 650 per year for all nationalities. Late renewal incurs SAR 500/month in penalties. Check your expiry date with our Iqama tracker.
