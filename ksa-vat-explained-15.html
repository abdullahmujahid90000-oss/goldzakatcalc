KSA VAT Explained (15%)

Saudi Arabia applies a Value Added Tax (VAT) of 15%. Two common price formats are used:

  • Exclusive: VAT is added on top of the base price.
  • Inclusive: VAT is already included; remove VAT to find the base price.

Formulas

  • Exclusive → Total = Base × 1.15
  • Inclusive → Base = Total ÷ 1.15
