How to Calculate VAT in Saudi Arabia?

Since July 2020, the standard VAT rate in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is 15%. This applies to most goods and services.

  • To add VAT: Multiply your amount by 1.15.
  • To find the VAT amount only: Multiply by 0.15.
  • To remove VAT: Divide the total price by 1.15.
