Car Financing in Saudi Arabia (2026 Guide)

Whether you are looking for a Lexus, Toyota, or the latest Changan models in Riyadh, understanding your monthly budget is the first step. Most banks like SNB, Al Rajhi, and Riyad Bank offer financing up to 5 years.

Note on Balloon Payments: Many dealerships in KSA offer a "30% or 40% Final Payment" option. This keeps your monthly installments low, but you must pay the large amount at the end of the 5th year.
