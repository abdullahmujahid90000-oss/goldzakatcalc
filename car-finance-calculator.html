📌 Saudi Car Finance: Most Saudi banks offer Islamic Murabaha financing (bank buys car, sells to you at marked-up price paid monthly). This is functionally similar to conventional interest loans. Typical bank rates: Al Rajhi 4–5%, Saudi National Bank 4–6%, Riyad Bank 3.9–5%.
