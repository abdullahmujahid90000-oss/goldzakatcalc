Comprehensive Guide to Car Ownership in KSA (2026)

This calculator is designed for all vehicle types, from economy sedans to luxury SUVs. Whether you are buying a brand-new model from an agency or a reliable used car from Haraj or a showroom, this tool helps you budget accurately.

1. Insurance Requirements

In Saudi Arabia, if you finance a car, banks require Comprehensive Insurance. Our calculator estimates this at roughly 4% of the car's value annually. For used cars, insurance costs may vary based on the vehicle's age and condition.

2. Financing Used Cars

Banks like Al Rajhi, SNB, and Riyad Bank now offer robust programs for used cars. Note that used car financing usually has a shorter maximum term (typically 3 years) and slightly higher profit rates compared to new cars.
