Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to send SAR to PKR?▾
Wise offers the best exchange rate. STC Pay is best for zero-fee small transfers. Al Rajhi is best for cash pickup across Pakistan.
How much SAR is 1 lakh PKR?▾
At ~74.5 rate, 1 lakh PKR = approximately SAR 1,342.
Can I send money instantly from Saudi to Pakistan?▾
Yes. STC Pay and Wise are near-instant. Western Union offers immediate cash pickup.
