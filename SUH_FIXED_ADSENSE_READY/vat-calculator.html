Frequently Asked Questions

What is the VAT rate in Saudi Arabia 2026?+
The standard VAT rate is 15%, in effect since 1 July 2020. Residential rent, healthcare, education and exports are exempt or zero-rated.
How do I calculate 15% VAT?+
To add: multiply by 1.15 (e.g. SAR 1,000 × 1.15 = SAR 1,150). To remove: divide by 1.15 (e.g. SAR 1,150 ÷ 1.15 = SAR 1,000).
Is there income tax in Saudi Arabia?+
No personal income tax in Saudi Arabia. The main taxes are VAT (15%) on consumption and Zakat (2.5%) on Muslim business wealth.
