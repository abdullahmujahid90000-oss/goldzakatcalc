🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Applies to saudiutilityhub.com

Saudi Utility Hub ("we", "our", "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly how we handle your data when you use our free calculators and read our blog.

1. What Data We Collect

Data you enter into calculators

All calculations run entirely inside your browser. Salary figures, dates, and other inputs you type into our calculators are never sent to our servers and are not stored anywhere. When you close or refresh the page, the data is gone.

Automatic data collected by Google services

We use Google AdSense (advertising) and Google Analytics (traffic analysis). These services collect:

  • Your approximate IP address and location (country/city level)
  • Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at the site (search, social, direct)
  • Cookies placed by Google on your device

This data is collected by Google under Google's own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

2. Cookies

We use cookies in two ways:

  • Essential cookies: A single cookie stores your cookie consent preference (whether you accepted or declined non-essential cookies). This is required for the site to remember your choice.
  • Google AdSense cookies: When you consent, Google sets cookies to serve personalised advertisements. These cookies may follow your activity across websites to show relevant ads. If you decline, ads may still appear but will not be personalised.

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Deleting cookies will reset your consent preference and you will see the consent banner again.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

Saudi Utility Hub displays Google AdSense advertisements. Google uses cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com or aboutads.info.

4. Affiliate Links & Commissions

Our remittance comparison pages contain affiliate links to services such as Wise, Remitly, and Western Union. If you click one of these links and sign up for the service, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions help us keep the site free.

Affiliate relationships never influence our calculator results, rankings, or editorial content. All sponsored content is clearly labelled.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data shared with Google is governed by Google's privacy policy. We do not have access to the individual-level data Google collects.

6. Children's Privacy

Saudi Utility Hub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@saudiutilityhub.com.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data held by third parties (such as Google Analytics). Contact Google directly to exercise these rights regarding their data collection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page, with the updated date shown at the top. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns: hello@saudiutilityhub.com
We aim to respond within 48 hours.
