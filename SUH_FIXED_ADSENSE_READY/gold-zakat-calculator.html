Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Nisab for gold Zakat 2026?+
85 grams of pure 24K gold. At May 2026 prices (~SAR 320/gram), Nisab ≈ SAR 27,200. Changes daily with gold prices.
Is Zakat 2.5% of the weight or the value?+
2.5% of the market VALUE (SAR amount), not the weight. Multiply weight by current price per gram, then calculate 2.5%.
Does Zakat apply to gold jewellery?+
Scholarly difference of opinion. Hanafi: yes on all gold. Hanbali (Saudi): generally exempt for personal use jewellery. Consult a scholar or alifta.gov.sa.
