Frequently Asked Questions

Is the dependent levy the same as Iqama renewal?+
No — they are completely separate. Iqama renewal (SAR 650/year) renews the residency document. Dependent levy (SAR 400/month per person) is an ongoing monthly charge.
Who pays the dependent levy?+
Saudi law puts the obligation on the employer/kafeel, but many contracts transfer the cost to the employee. Check your contract before bringing family.
Who is exempt from dependent levy?+
Dependents of Saudi citizens. Dependents on their own work visas. Some government employee dependents.
