Frequently Asked Questions

What is the overtime rate in Saudi Arabia?+
Under Saudi Labour Law Article 107, overtime is paid at 150% of the regular hourly rate. This means for every extra hour worked, you earn 1.5x your normal hourly pay.
How is the hourly rate calculated in Saudi Arabia?+
Basic salary ÷ (working days per month × working hours per day). For example: SAR 8,000 ÷ (26 days × 8 hours) = SAR 38.46 per hour.
Is transport allowance included in overtime calculation?+
No. Overtime is calculated on basic salary only, not housing or transport allowances.
