How to Use Wise from Saudi Arabia

  1. Create a Wise account with your passport and Iqama number
  2. Enter SAR amount and select PKR destination
  3. Add recipient's HBL, MCB, Meezan, UBL, or any Pakistani bank account (IBAN format)
  4. Pay via Saudi bank transfer or Mada/Visa debit card
  5. PKR arrives in 1–2 business days

Tips to Send the Most PKR from Saudi Arabia

  • Compare on the same day — exchange rates change daily, sometimes significantly
  • Send on weekdays — banks often widen spreads on Fridays and weekends
  • Bank-to-bank beats cash — bank transfers almost always get better rates than cash pickup
  • Use first-time offers — Remitly and Wise frequently offer zero-fee or boosted-rate promotions for new users
  • Combine transfers — flat fees eat less into larger amounts (send SAR 3,000 once rather than SAR 1,000 three times)
