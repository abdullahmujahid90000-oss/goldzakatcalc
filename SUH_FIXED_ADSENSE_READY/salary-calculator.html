How the Calculator Works

For Saudi nationals: GOSI is 9.75% of (basic salary + housing allowance), capped at SAR 45,000/month. No income tax. For expat employees: zero deductions — gross equals net.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there income tax in Saudi Arabia?+
No. Saudi Arabia has no personal income tax for anyone — Saudi nationals or expats. The only mandatory deduction is GOSI (9.75%) for Saudi employees.
What is included in the GOSI base?+
GOSI is calculated on basic salary + housing allowance combined, up to SAR 45,000/month. Transport, phone and other allowances are excluded.
Do expats pay GOSI?+
No. Expat employees pay zero GOSI. Employers pay 2% Occupational Hazards only — this does not affect your take-home pay.
What is the maximum GOSI deduction in 2026?+
SAR 45,000 × 9.75% = SAR 4,387.50/month maximum. Salary above the cap is not subject to GOSI.
