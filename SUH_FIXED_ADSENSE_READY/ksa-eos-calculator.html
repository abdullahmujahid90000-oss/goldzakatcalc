Frequently Asked Questions

Does housing allowance count in EOS?+
No. EOS is always calculated on basic salary only. Housing, transport and all other allowances are excluded.
Do expats get EOS in Saudi Arabia?+
Yes. Both Saudi nationals and expats are entitled to EOS gratuity under Saudi Labour Law after completing at least 2 years.
How long does the employer have to pay EOS?+
7 days if you resigned. Immediately if employer terminated you. Late payment can be enforced through hrsd.gov.sa.
