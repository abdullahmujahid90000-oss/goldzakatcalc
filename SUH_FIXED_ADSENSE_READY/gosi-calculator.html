Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GOSI rate in 2026?+
Saudi employees pay 9.75%: 9% pension + 0.75% SANED. Employers pay an additional 11.75%. Cap is SAR 45,000/month.
Do expats pay GOSI in Saudi Arabia?+
No. Expats pay 0% GOSI. Employers pay 2% Occupational Hazards only — not deducted from salary.
Is transport allowance included in GOSI?+
No. Only basic salary + housing allowance, up to SAR 45,000/month.
