Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Iqama renewal cost in 2026?+
SAR 650 per year. Late renewal adds SAR 500 per month overdue. 3 months late = SAR 650 + SAR 1,500 = SAR 2,150 total.
How do I renew my Iqama?+
Via Absher app or absher.sa. Login with your Iqama number, pay SAR 650 via SADAD or bank card, receive instant confirmation.
Can I travel with an expired Iqama?+
No. You cannot get an exit visa with an expired Iqama. Renew first before any international travel.
