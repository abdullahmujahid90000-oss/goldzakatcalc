❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest way to send money from Saudi Arabia to Pakistan?
As of June 2026, Al Rajhi Bank offers the lowest total cost for SAR→PKR transfers under SAR 2,000, with a rate of ~75.5 PKR/SAR and no fee. For larger amounts, STC Pay and Wise provide competitive rates.
Which provider is fastest for SAR to India?
STC Pay and Al Rajhi offer instant transfers to major Indian banks (within 10 minutes). Western Union takes 15–30 minutes for cash pickup.
Do remittance providers charge hidden fees?
Yes. Many advertise 'zero fee' but apply a poor exchange rate (margin of 2–5%). Always check the final 'receiver gets' amount, not just the fee.
Is it better to use a bank or money-transfer operator?
For small amounts (SAR 5,000), banks like Al Rajhi or SNB offer better rates and lower margins.
What documents are needed to send money from KSA?
Valid Iqama or Saudi ID, recipient's full name, bank account number (or mobile number for wallet transfers), and purpose of transfer (required by SAMA).
