❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest way to send money from Saudi Arabia to Pakistan?
Two options send to Pakistan with no transfer fee: Barq (no fee on any amount) and Tahweel Al Rajhi via its app, ATM or call centre (free to Pakistan regardless of amount). Even with a zero fee, compare the exchange rate in each app, because the rate decides how many rupees your family receives.
Which provider is fastest for SAR to India?
App-based wallets like Barq, urpay, STC Pay and Tahweel Al Rajhi usually credit major Indian banks within minutes. Western Union and Enjaz cash pickup is typically ready in 15–30 minutes.
Do remittance providers charge hidden fees?
Yes. Many advertise 'zero fee' but apply a poor exchange rate (margin of 2–5%). Always check the final 'receiver gets' amount, not just the fee.
Is it better to use a bank or money-transfer operator?
Newer fintech apps such as Barq and Tiqmo often have the lowest fees, while bank-backed services like Tahweel Al Rajhi and Enjaz offer wider branch networks and cash pickup. For most expats an app transfer is cheapest; always compare the final received amount before sending.
What documents are needed to send money from KSA?
Valid Iqama or Saudi ID, recipient's full name, bank account number (or mobile number for wallet transfers), and purpose of transfer (required by SAMA).
