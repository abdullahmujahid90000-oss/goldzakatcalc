Key Transfer Tips:

📉 Watch the Exchange Rate

A low fee with a bad exchange rate can cost you more than a high fee with a good rate. Always compare the "amount received".

⏱️ Speed vs Cost

Instant transfers (Wallets/WU) cost more. Standard bank transfers (1-3 days) are cheaper. Plan ahead to save money.

🔒 Security

Always use licensed providers regulated by SAMA (Saudi Central Bank). Avoid unlicensed exchange houses.

Important: Remittance limits apply. Usually SAR 10,000 per transaction via apps. Verify your daily limit with your bank.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Remittance

What is the cheapest way to send money from Saudi Arabia?
Digital wallets like STC Pay and Alinma Bank's app often offer the lowest fees (SAR 5-10) compared to traditional Western Union (SAR 20+) or branch transfers. Bank-to-bank transfers within Saudi Arabia are free, but international transfers vary.
How much does it cost to send money to the Philippines from Saudi?
Fees range from SAR 5 to SAR 25 depending on the provider. STC Pay and Al Rajhi often have promotions around SAR 5-8. Western Union is faster but costs SAR 20-25. Exchange rates also affect the final amount received.
Is it better to use a bank or an app for remittance?
Apps are generally faster and cheaper for small amounts. Banks are better for large amounts (SAR 10,000+) due to safety and sometimes better exchange rates, though fees might be higher.
Are there daily limits for remittance in Saudi Arabia?
Yes, SAMA (Saudi Central Bank) sets limits. Usually SAR 10,000 per transaction via apps, up to SAR 50,000 per day via bank branches. Limits vary by provider and account type.
Which bank has the best exchange rate in Saudi Arabia?
Exchange rates fluctuate daily. Al Rajhi, SNB (AlAhli), and Alinma often offer competitive rates. It is best to compare the 'effective rate' (rate after fees) rather than just the transfer fee.
Can I send money without a Saudi bank account?
Yes, services like Western Union, MoneyGram, and some exchange houses allow cash-to-cash transfers without a local bank account, though fees are usually higher.
How long does a bank transfer from Saudi Arabia take?
App transfers usually arrive in minutes. Bank-to-bank transfers can take 1-3 business days depending on the destination country and intermediary banks.
What documents are needed for remittance?
For app transfers, just your ID/Iqama. For large branch transfers, you may need your Iqama, salary certificate, and proof of relationship to the beneficiary.
