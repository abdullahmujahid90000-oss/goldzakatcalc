Who Built This?

Saudi Utility Hub was created by a team of finance and HR professionals based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We noticed that most expats in KSA — especially from Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, and the Philippines — struggled to find free, accurate, and Saudi-specific tools for everyday financial decisions.

We built what we wished existed: a single, free platform covering every calculation an expat or resident in Saudi Arabia might need.

Our Data Sources

We only use official Saudi government and internationally recognised sources for our rates and rules:

  • 📌 GOSI rates & rules — gosi.gov.sa
  • 📌 VAT regulations — zatca.gov.sa (ZATCA)
  • 📌 Labour Law / EOS — Saudi Ministry of Human Resources (hrsd.gov.sa)
  • 📌 Iqama / visa fees — General Directorate of Passports (jawazat.gov.sa) / Absher
  • 📌 Dependent levy — Ministry of Finance official fee schedules
  • 📌 Gold prices — Saudi gold market live feeds
  • 📌 Remittance rates — Live feeds from Wise, Al Rajhi, STC Pay, WU, Remitly apps

Accuracy & Disclaimer

All calculations on Saudi Utility Hub are estimates based on publicly available rates and Saudi government regulations as of the date shown on each page. While we update rates regularly and cross-check against official sources, regulations change. Always verify critical financial decisions with your employer, HR department, bank, or official Saudi authorities:

  • GOSI: gosi.gov.sa
  • ZATCA (VAT): zatca.gov.sa
  • Labour Law: hrsd.gov.sa
  • Jawazat (Iqama/Visa): jawazat.gov.sa
