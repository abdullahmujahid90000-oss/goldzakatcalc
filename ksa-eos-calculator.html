How is End of Service Calculated?

According to **Article 84** of the Saudi Labor Law, the reward is calculated as follows:

  • First 5 Years: Half a month's salary for each year.
  • After 5 Years: One full month's salary for each additional year.

What if you Resign? (Article 85)

If you resign, the amount depends on your total service:

  • Less than 2 years: **No reward.**
  • 2 to 5 years: **1/3 of the reward.**
  • 5 to 10 years: **2/3 of the reward.**
  • Over 10 years: **Full reward.**
