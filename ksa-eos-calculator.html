Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EOS formula in Saudi Arabia 2026? ▼
Under Saudi Labour Law Article 84: ½ month basic salary per year for the first 5 years, then 1 full month per year thereafter. Resignation reduces the entitlement for service under 10 years. Termination by employer = full EOS always.
Does housing allowance affect EOS? ▼
No. EOS is calculated on basic salary only. Housing allowance, transport, and all other benefits are excluded from the EOS calculation.
How long does the employer have to pay EOS? ▼
If you resigned: employer must pay within 7 days. If employer terminated you: payment must be immediate. Late payment can be enforced through the Labour Court or Ministry of Human Resources (hrsd.gov.sa).
