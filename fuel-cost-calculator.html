вќ“ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current petrol price in Saudi Arabia 2026?

As of Q2 2026: Premium 91 = SAR 0.67/litre, Super 95 = SAR 0.90/litre, Diesel = SAR 0.32/litre. Prices are set by Saudi Aramco quarterly (January, April, July, October) based on international oil prices.

Should I use 91 or 95 octane petrol in Saudi Arabia?

Check your car manual. Most modern cars and turbocharged engines require 95 octane minimum. Using 91 in a 95-rated engine can cause engine knocking and long-term damage. Older and simpler engines (many Japanese sedans made before 2015) run fine on 91 octane and it saves SAR 0.23/litre.

How often do Saudi Arabia fuel prices change?

Saudi Aramco reviews and potentially adjusts fuel prices every quarter вЂ” in January, April, July, and October. Prices are linked to global oil markets but remain among the lowest in the world due to government policy on energy subsidies.

Is diesel cheaper than petrol in Saudi Arabia?

Yes, significantly. Diesel costs only SAR 0.32/litre вЂ” less than half the price of 91 octane. Diesel is heavily subsidised in Saudi Arabia and is the most economical fuel for trucks, large SUVs, and commercial vehicles.

Where can I find petrol stations in Saudi Arabia?

Saudi Aramco operates petrol stations under the SAPCO brand across KSA. Other brands include Al-Baik affiliated stations, independent local stations, and international brands. All sell the same Aramco-set fuel prices вЂ” there is no price variation between stations.
