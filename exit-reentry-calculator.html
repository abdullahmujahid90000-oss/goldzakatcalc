❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the exit re-entry visa fee in Saudi Arabia?
The exit re-entry visa fee in Saudi Arabia is SAR 200 for single entry or SAR 500 for multiple entries as of 2026.
How long is exit re-entry visa valid?
Single exit re-entry is valid for up to 2 months from issue date. Multiple exit re-entry is valid for up to 3 months from issue date.
Do I need exit re-entry for dependents?
Yes, each dependent (spouse, children) needs their own exit re-entry visa at the same fee as the primary holder.
Can I extend exit re-entry visa?
No, exit re-entry visas cannot be extended. You must return to Saudi Arabia before expiry or apply for a new visa.
What happens if I overstay exit re-entry?
If you overstay your exit re-entry visa, you'll face a SAR 1,000 fine and may be banned from re-entering Saudi Arabia.
How to apply for exit re-entry visa?
Apply through Absher portal under 'My Services' → 'Passports' → 'Visa Services' → 'Exit Re-Entry Visa'.
Is there a grace period after expiry?
No, there is no grace period for exit re-entry visas. You must return before the expiry date printed on your visa.
Can I get exit re-entry after final exit?
No, once you complete final exit, your Iqama is cancelled. You would need a new work visa from your employer.
