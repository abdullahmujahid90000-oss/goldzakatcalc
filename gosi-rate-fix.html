• Saudi employee contribution: 9.75% of (basic salary + housing allowance), capped at SAR 45,000
  • Breakdown: 9% Pension (Annuities) + 0.75% SANED (Unemployment Insurance)
  • Employer contribution: 11.75% (9% pension + 0.75% SANED + 2% Occupational Hazards)
  • Non-Saudi (expat) employees: 0% employee contribution. Employer pays 2% for Occupational Hazards only
  • GOSI cap: contributions are not calculated on salary above SAR 45,000/month
