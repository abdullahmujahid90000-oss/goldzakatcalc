📋 Ejar — Saudi Rental Registration Guide

Ejar (إيجار) is Saudi Arabia's official rental registration platform. All rental contracts must be registered on Ejar. Without Ejar registration, the contract is not legally binding and you cannot use it for Iqama renewal, bank applications or residency purposes.

1

Landlord registers on Ejar: The landlord (or their agent) creates the contract on ejar.sa with the property details, rent amount and duration.

2

Tenant verifies via Absher: You receive an SMS notification and approve the contract via Absher or the Ejar app. Your Iqama number is linked to the contract.

3

Contract is activated: Once both parties sign digitally, the Ejar contract is issued with an official contract number. Keep this — you'll need it for Iqama renewal and bank applications.

4

Payments via SADAD: Rent payments linked to the contract can be set up via SADAD bill payment. Cheques are still used but SADAD is increasingly common for annual rent.

⚠️ Unregistered contracts: If your landlord refuses to register on Ejar, this is a red flag. You have fewer legal protections and cannot use the contract for official purposes. Report issues to ejar.sa helpline.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard housing allowance in Saudi Arabia?
The industry standard is 25–30% of basic salary per month. For example, if your basic salary is SAR 8,000, a typical housing allowance is SAR 2,000–2,400/month. Some companies provide it as a percentage; others fix it at a flat amount. Government sector typically provides 30%.
Can I negotiate for a higher housing allowance?
Yes. Housing allowance is negotiable at the time of job offer or during contract renewal. If the housing allowance in your offer doesn't cover typical rent in your city, negotiate before signing. Once the contract is signed, it's harder to change without a formal salary review.
Is housing allowance taxable or included in EOS calculation?
There is no income tax in Saudi Arabia. Housing allowance is NOT included in EOS gratuity calculation (EOS uses basic salary only). However, housing allowance IS included in the daily leave encashment rate calculation.
What is Ejar and is it mandatory?
Ejar (إيجار) is Saudi Arabia's official rental contract registration platform. Since 2017, all residential rental agreements in Saudi Arabia must be registered on Ejar. An unregistered contract is not legally enforceable and cannot be used for official purposes (Iqama renewal, bank applications).
