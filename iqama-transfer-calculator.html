Understanding Iqama Transfers in Saudi Arabia (2026 Complete Guide)

Changing employers in Saudi Arabia requires an official Iqama (residency permit) transfer through the Qiwa platform. This process updates your sponsorship from your current employer to a new company while maintaining your legal residency status. Our free calculator helps you estimate transfer costs, understand Nitaqat eligibility requirements, and clarify payment responsibilities based on official Ministry of Human Resources (HRSD) regulations for 2026.

💰 Current Transfer Fees & Rules

  • Government Transfer Fee: SAR 2,000 — Paid via Qiwa to process the sponsorship change
  • Payment Responsibility: Legally, employers should cover government fees. However, many new companies require employees to pay as part of the hiring agreement. Always clarify this before signing your new contract.
  • Nitaqat Compliance: Transfers are only allowed to companies in Green or Platinum ranges. Red Nitaqat companies cannot accept new expat employees.

⚖️ Legal Transfer Conditions:

  • Contract Completion: Easiest after contract ends. Mid-contract requires mutual agreement or HRSD approval.
  • Salary Arrears: If current employer fails to pay salary for 3 months, you can transfer without sponsor consent via Qiwa.
  • Expired Iqama: If sponsor failed to renew an expired Iqama, you can transfer immediately without penalty.

⏱️ Processing Time: Typically 1-3 days with sponsor approval. Up to 2 weeks if contested or requires Ministry intervention. Platinum companies often process within 24 hours.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Iqama Transfer

How much does it cost to transfer Iqama in 2026?+

Standard government fee is SAR 2,000. Payment responsibility depends on the new employment agreement.

Can I transfer without sponsor consent?+

Yes, if contract ended, Iqama expired & sponsor failed to renew, or employer didn't pay salary for 3 months.

What are the Nitaqat requirements?+

New employer must be Green or Platinum. Red companies cannot accept transfers. Check real-time status on Qiwa.

How long does transfer take?+

24-48 hours with sponsor approval. 1-2 weeks if contested. Platinum companies often process within 24 hours.
