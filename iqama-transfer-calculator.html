❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Iqama Transfer

How much does it cost to transfer Iqama in Saudi Arabia 2026?
The standard government fee for Iqama transfer is SAR 2,000. Additional costs may apply if the new employer pays for the transfer, or if there are premium Nitaqat requirements. The employee typically does not pay the government fee unless agreed upon.
Can an employee transfer their Iqama without sponsor consent?
Under certain conditions, yes. If the contract has ended, the sponsor failed to renew the Iqama, or the employer failed to pay salary for 3 months, the employee can transfer via the Qiwa platform without the sponsor's direct approval.
What are the Nitaqat requirements for Iqama transfer?
The new employer must be in the Green or Platinum Nitaqat range to accept transfers. Red Nitaqat companies cannot hire new expats. High Green companies have fewer restrictions. Check the Qiwa portal for real-time Nitaqat status.
Who pays the Iqama transfer fee?
Legally, the employer is responsible for government fees. However, in practice, many new employers require the employee to cover the SAR 2,000 transfer fee as part of the hiring agreement. This should be clarified in the offer letter.
How long does an Iqama transfer take?
If the current sponsor approves, the transfer can be completed within 24-48 hours via Qiwa. If the transfer is contested or requires Ministry approval, it may take 1-2 weeks.
Is there a limit on how many times I can transfer my Iqama?
There is no legal limit on the number of transfers. However, frequent transfers may raise questions during visa stamping or renewals. Ensure each transfer is documented with a valid employment contract.
What happens to my Iqama validity after transfer?
The Iqama validity remains the same as the original expiry date. The new sponsor is responsible for renewing it before expiration. You do not get a new validity period automatically upon transfer.
Can I transfer my Iqama if I have unpaid traffic fines?
Yes, traffic fines are linked to the individual, not the sponsor. You can transfer your Iqama even with unpaid fines, but you must clear them to renew the Iqama or get an exit/re-entry visa later.
