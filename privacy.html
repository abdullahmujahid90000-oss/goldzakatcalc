🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Saudi Utility Hub ("we", "us", or "our") operates https://www.saudiutilityhub.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the Site.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal information through our calculators. All calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers.

However, we do use third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • Google AdSense: Collects non-personal data (IP address, browser type, device info) for ad targeting
  • Google Analytics: Tracks page views, session duration, and traffic sources (anonymized)
  • Cookies: Store consent preferences and ad performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data only for:

  • Providing and maintaining our calculators
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyzing site traffic to improve user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preferences
  • Measure ad performance and analytics
  • Ensure site functionality

You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie banner. Declining cookies will disable ads and analytics but won't affect calculator functionality.

4. Google AdSense Disclosure

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our Site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and other sites on the Internet.

Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data through calculators, there's minimal risk to your privacy. Third-party services (Google) implement industry-standard security measures to protect collected data.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (though we hold none from calculators)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection by declining cookies
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: saudiutilityhub@gmail.com
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

By clicking "Accept" on our cookie banner, you consent to our use of cookies for ads and analytics as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies or contacting us.
