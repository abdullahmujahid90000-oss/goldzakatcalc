Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to Saudi Utility Hub. We respect your right to privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while delivering accurate, free tools for expatriates and residents in Saudi Arabia. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share data when you interact with our calculators, guides, and website services. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate, improve, and secure our services:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, calculator inputs (e.g., salary figures, expiry dates), and device/browser information. All inputs are processed locally in your browser and are never stored on our servers.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand traffic patterns. Third-party advertising cookies are used via Google AdSense to display relevant ads.
  • Contact Information: If you email us or submit a query, we retain your email address and message solely for customer support and policy updates.

🍪 Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

Saudi Utility Hub participates in the Google AdSense program. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites on the internet. These cookies enable interest-based advertising and help us measure ad performance. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or NAI Opt-Out Page. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

🔐 How We Use & Protect Your Data

Your data is used strictly to:

  • ✅ Improve calculator accuracy and user experience
  • ✅ Analyze traffic trends and fix technical issues
  • ✅ Deliver relevant, non-intrusive advertisements via AdSense
  • ✅ Respond to support inquiries and policy notifications

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, regular security audits, and strict access controls. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

📋 Your Rights & Data Control

  1. Access & Correction: Request a copy of any personal data we hold or correct inaccuracies by contacting privacy@saudiutilityhub.com.
  2. Deletion Request: Ask us to erase your contact information or support history. Calculator inputs cannot be recovered as they are not stored.
  3. Opt-Out: Disable cookies via your browser settings or use the banner below. Note: Disabling cookies may limit site functionality.
  4. Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw consent for analytics tracking at any time without affecting core calculator features.

❓ Privacy Policy FAQs

Do you store my calculator inputs?+

No. All calculations run locally in your browser. We never transmit salary, Iqama, or fee data to our servers.

How do I disable AdSense cookies?+

Click "Decline" on the cookie banner, or visit Google Ad Settings to opt out of personalized ads.

Do you share my email address?+

No. Emails are used only for support replies and policy updates. We never sell or share contact details.

Is my data safe on public devices?+

Yes, but we recommend using private browsing mode and clearing cache after use on shared computers or kiosks.
