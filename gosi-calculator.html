Frequently Asked Questions

Do expats pay GOSI?▾
No. Expats don't pay GOSI. Employers pay a 2% work hazard insurance for expats. Saudi nationals pay 10% employee contribution.
What is the GOSI salary ceiling?▾
SAR 45,000/month. Contributions are capped at this amount regardless of actual salary.
What does GOSI provide?▾
Old-age pension, disability benefits, work injury compensation and survivors' benefits for Saudi nationals.
