Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GOSI contribution rate for Saudi employees in 2026? ▼
Saudi employee: 9.75% (9% pension annuities + 0.75% SANED). This is deducted from your monthly salary. Employer pays an additional 11.75%. Maximum base: SAR 45,000/month.
Do expat employees pay GOSI? ▼
No. Non-Saudi employees pay zero GOSI. Their employer pays only 2% Occupational Hazards insurance, which does not affect the employee's salary.
Is transport allowance included in GOSI? ▼
No. Transport allowance is excluded from the GOSI base. Only basic salary and housing allowance are included, subject to the SAR 45,000 monthly cap.
