Calculate employee (9.75%) and employer (12%) GOSI contributions with SAR 45,000 salary cap. Free, accurate, official rates.

In Saudi Arabia 2026, employees pay 9.75% GOSI on basic salary (capped at SAR 45,000 = max SAR 4,387.50), while employers pay 12% (max SAR 5,400). Total monthly GOSI = 21.75% of capped basic salary.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GOSI employee contribution rate in 2026?
The GOSI employee contribution rate in Saudi Arabia is 9.75% of basic salary, capped at SAR 45,000 per month.
What is the GOSI employer contribution rate?
Employers must contribute 12% of the employee's basic salary to GOSI, also capped at SAR 45,000.
Does GOSI apply to expatriate workers?
Yes, GOSI applies to all private sector employees in Saudi Arabia, including expatriates. Both Saudi and foreign workers pay 9.75%.
Is housing allowance included in GOSI calculation?
No. Only basic salary is subject to GOSI. Housing, transport, and other allowances are exempt.
What is the maximum GOSI deduction?
The maximum employee GOSI deduction is SAR 4,387.50 (9.75% of SAR 45,000). Employer max is SAR 5,400 (12% of SAR 45,000).
How to check GOSI contributions?
Employees can check their GOSI contributions through the Qiwa portal (qiwa.sa) or the GOSI mobile app.
Can I get a GOSI refund?
Expatriate workers may be eligible for a GOSI refund upon final exit from Saudi Arabia, provided they meet eligibility criteria.
What happens if my employer doesn't pay GOSI?
You can report non-compliance to the Ministry of Human Resources via the Qiwa platform or Absher.
