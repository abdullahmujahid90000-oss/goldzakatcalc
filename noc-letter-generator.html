📊 Required Elements for Saudi NOC Letters

According to Ministry of Human Resources guidelines, a valid NOC letter in Saudi Arabia must include:

  • Company Letterhead: Official company logo, name, address, and contact information
  • Employee Details: Full name, Iqama number, nationality, and passport number
  • Purpose Statement: Clear statement of what the NOC is for (transfer, family visit, etc.)
  • No Objection Clause: Explicit statement that the employer has no objection to the request
  • Company Authorization: Signature of authorized signatory, company stamp, and date
  • Language: Arabic or bilingual format for official acceptance

⚖️ Article 39 of Saudi Labour Law

Under Article 39 of Saudi Labour Law, employees have the right to transfer employers without NOC if they meet these conditions:

  • Completed Contract: You've finished your fixed-term contract period
  • Unlimited Contract: You've worked for at least 1 year under an unlimited contract
  • No Violations: You haven't violated any contract terms or labour laws

If you meet these conditions, your employer cannot legally prevent your transfer, even without providing NOC.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a NOC letter in Saudi Arabia?
A NOC (No Objection Certificate) is an official document from your employer stating they have no objection to your specific request, such as visa transfer, family visit sponsorship, or final exit.
When do I need a NOC letter in KSA?
You need a NOC letter for: transferring to a new employer, sponsoring family members for visit visas, final exit procedures, or changing job categories under the same sponsor.
Is NOC mandatory for visa transfer?
Under Article 39 of Saudi Labour Law, NOC is not required if you've completed your contract or worked for 1 year with an unlimited contract. However, many employers still provide it voluntarily.
What should be included in a Saudi NOC letter?
A Saudi NOC letter should include: company letterhead, employee details (name, Iqama), purpose of NOC, statement of no objection, company stamp/signature, and date.
Can I generate my own NOC letter?
You can create a draft using our generator, but it must be printed on official company letterhead and signed/stamped by your employer to be valid.
Is Arabic required for NOC letters?
For official purposes in Saudi Arabia, NOC letters should be in Arabic or bilingual (Arabic/English). English-only letters may not be accepted by government agencies.
What if my employer refuses to provide NOC?
If you meet Article 39 conditions (completed contract or 1+ year service), your employer cannot legally refuse transfer. File a complaint with Ministry of Human Resources through Qiwa or Absher.
How long is a NOC letter valid?
NOC letters don't have an official expiry date, but most government agencies prefer letters issued within the last 30–90 days.
