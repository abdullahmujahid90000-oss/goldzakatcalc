ℹ️ About Saudi Utility Hub

Our Mission

Saudi Utility Hub was founded in 2025 to solve a simple problem: accurate, free, and easy-to-use tools for life in Saudi Arabia.

Whether you're a Saudi national, an expatriate worker, or a business owner, navigating GOSI, EOS, Zakat, Iqama, or remittance fees can be confusing. Official government portals often lack calculators, and third-party sites use outdated or incorrect data.

We build one-page tools that combine:

  • ✅ Official 2026 rates from SAMA, ZATCA, GOSI, Ministry of HR, and alifta.gov.sa
  • ✅ Real-world examples (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam scenarios)
  • ✅ Sharia-compliant guidance reviewed by Saudi Islamic scholars
  • ✅ No login, no ads in calculators — just instant answers

Who We Are

We are a small team of developers, financial analysts, and Islamic finance specialists based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Our work is guided by three principles:

  1. Accuracy First: Every number is verified against official Saudi sources. If a rate changes (e.g., GOSI 9.75%, VAT 15%, Dependent Levy SAR 400), we update it within 24 hours.
  2. User Trust: We never sell user data. Calculators run entirely in your browser — no tracking during calculation.
  3. E-E-A-T Compliance: Each tool is reviewed by a topic-matched expert before publishing (e.g., labour law by HR specialists, Zakat by scholars).

Our Review Process

Every calculator and guide undergoes a 3-step review:

  1. Data Validation: Cross-check with at least two official Saudi government sources.
  2. Expert Review: Assigned to a credentialed reviewer (see team below).
  3. Monthly Audit: All pages are re-verified on the 1st of each month for rate changes.
