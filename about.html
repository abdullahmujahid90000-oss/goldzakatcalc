Our Mission

Saudi Utility Hub was built to solve a simple problem: people living in Saudi Arabia need accurate, free tools for everyday financial decisions — and most don't exist in one place.

Whether you're calculating your GOSI deduction, figuring out your end-of-service gratuity, checking your Iqama expiry, or comparing remittance fees, we give you the answer instantly — based on the official rules, not estimates.

  • ✅ Official 2026 rates from SAMA, ZATCA, GOSI, and the Ministry of Human Resources
  • ✅ No login required — every tool works instantly in your browser
  • ✅ Calculations stay on your device — we do not store what you enter
  • ✅ Monthly audits — all rates are re-verified on the 1st of each month

What We Cover

Saudi Utility Hub covers six core areas for residents and expats in the Kingdom:

  • 💼 Salary & Work — GOSI, EOS, salary slips, settlements, overtime, leave, probation
  • 🧾 Tax & Zakat — VAT at 15%, complete Zakat calculator, Gold Zakat, Nisab
  • 🏦 Loans & Banking — car finance, mortgage, Islamic finance (Murabaha), IBAN validator
  • 💸 Send Money Home — remittance fee comparison for Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Philippines
  • 🪪 Iqama & Visas — expiry tracker, transfer costs, dependent levy, family visa, exit/re-entry
  • 🚗 Bills & Transport — electricity, water, fuel, traffic fines, driving licence conversion

Our Review Process

Every calculator and guide on this site follows a three-step process before it goes live:

  1. Data validation — cross-checked against at least two official Saudi government sources (GOSI, ZATCA, SAMA, Ministry of HR, Jawazat).
  2. Editorial review — assigned to the relevant desk based on topic (see below).
  3. Monthly audit — all pages re-verified on the 1st of each month for any rate or regulation changes.
