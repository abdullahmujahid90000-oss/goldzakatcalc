What is Saudi Utility Hub?

Saudi Utility Hub is a free tools and calculators platform built for expats, residents, and businesses in Saudi Arabia. We provide 23+ free calculators covering salary, GOSI, End of Service gratuity, Zakat, Iqama renewal, dependent levy, VAT, traffic fines, domestic worker costs, fuel, and remittance — in both English and Arabic.

Everything is free. No account. No subscription. No credit card. Every tool works entirely in your browser — nothing is ever sent to a server.

Our Mission

Millions of people from Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Egypt, the Philippines, Indonesia and beyond live and work in Saudi Arabia. Every year they face complex financial questions: How much Zakat do I owe on my gold? What is my EOS gratuity if I resign after 4 years? How much does my Iqama renewal cost if I'm 2 months late?

These questions deserve fast, accurate, free answers. Saudi Utility Hub exists to provide exactly that.
