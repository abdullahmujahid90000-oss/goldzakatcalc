Who Built This?

Saudi Utility Hub was created by a team of finance and HR professionals based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We noticed that millions of expats — from Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Egypt and the Philippines — struggled to find free, accurate, Saudi-specific tools for everyday financial decisions.

Most calculator sites are generic. Ours are built specifically for Saudi Arabia's unique rules: GOSI rates, Saudi Labour Law Article 84, ZATCA's 15% VAT, Jawazat Iqama fees — all cross-checked against official government sources before every update.

Why Free? How We Make Money

All tools are and always will be free. We cover operational costs through two transparent revenue streams: Google AdSense advertisements displayed on pages, and affiliate commissions when users sign up for services we compare (such as remittance platforms like Wise or Remitly).

📢 Advertising & Affiliate Disclosure: This site displays Google AdSense advertisements. We may earn affiliate commissions when you sign up for services through links on our site — such as Wise, Remitly, or Western Union. These commissions never influence our calculations, tool rankings, or editorial content. All sponsored placements are clearly labelled. We recommend services based on user value, not commission rates.

Accuracy & Disclaimer

All calculations on Saudi Utility Hub are estimates based on publicly available rates and Saudi government regulations as of the date shown on each page. While we update rates regularly and cross-check against official sources, regulations change frequently. Always verify critical financial decisions with your employer, HR department, bank, or official Saudi authorities:

GOSI: gosi.gov.sa  ·  VAT: zatca.gov.sa  ·  Labour Law: hrsd.gov.sa  ·  Jawazat: jawazat.gov.sa
