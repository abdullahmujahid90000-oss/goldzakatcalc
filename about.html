About Saudi Utility Hub 🇸🇦

Free, accurate calculators for everyone living and working in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Mission

Saudi Utility Hub was created with a single purpose: make accurate, practical financial and legal information freely accessible to everyone in Saudi Arabia — Saudi citizens, expat workers, business owners, and families alike.

Navigating Saudi labour law, Zakat calculations, Iqama rules, VAT, and remittance fees can be confusing and time-consuming. Our tools do the heavy lifting for you — for free, without signup, and in both Arabic and English.

What We Cover

Our tools span the most important needs for KSA residents:

Employment: Salary, End of Service (EOS/gratuity), GOSI, working hours, overtime, Musaned domestic workers.

Expat Life: Iqama expiry, dependent levy, exit re-entry visas, remittance comparisons, currency converter.

Zakat & Islamic Finance: Gold Zakat, comprehensive Zakat calculator, Nisab checker, current gold prices.

Tax & Finance: VAT calculator (15%), car finance, mortgage, fuel costs, traffic fines.

Health: BMI and ideal weight, pregnancy due date calculator.

Accuracy & Updates

All calculators are reviewed and updated at least annually to reflect the latest Saudi regulations. Key data points we track include: Saudi Labour Law amendments, GOSI contribution rates, VAT regulations (ZATCA), Aramco fuel prices, Nisab gold prices, and Musaned fee schedules.

If you notice an error or outdated information, please contact us and we will correct it promptly.
