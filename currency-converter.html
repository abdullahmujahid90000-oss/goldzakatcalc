❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the SAR exchange rate shown?

Rates are mid-market rates from public FX data, updated daily. Actual rates from banks or remittance providers will be slightly lower (they include a spread/markup, typically 0.5–3%).

Is SAR pegged to any currency?

Yes. The Saudi Riyal has been pegged to the US Dollar at SAR 3.75 = USD 1 since 1986. This means SAR/USD is stable, while other crosses move with the USD.

What's the best way to convert SAR to PKR or INR?

For PKR and INR, STC Pay, Al Rajhi Tahweel, and Wise consistently offer the best combined rate + fee. See our remittance comparison page for current totals.

Are these rates real-time?

Rates refresh daily. For real-time inter-bank rates, check XE or your bank's app — but for typical sending amounts, our daily rate is within 0.3% of real-time.
