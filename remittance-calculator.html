💡 Tips for Cheaper Transfers

1. Compare total cost — always add the fee AND the exchange rate margin, not just the headline fee.

2. Send larger amounts — many providers charge a flat fee, so sending SAR 2,000 vs 500 cuts your cost percentage in half.

3. Use bank-to-bank transfers — Al Rajhi Bank and SARIE offer competitive rates for popular corridors like India and Pakistan.

4. Check STC Pay and Urpay — digital wallets often have zero fees for some corridors.

5. Time your transfer — exchange rates fluctuate; transferring on weekdays usually gives better rates.
