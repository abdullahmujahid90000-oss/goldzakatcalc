Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the dependent levy in Saudi Arabia 2026? ▼
SAR 400 per dependent per month. Annual cost per dependent: SAR 4,800. Saudi citizen dependents are fully exempt. Dependents on their own work visas are not counted.
Who pays the dependent levy — employee or employer? ▼
Saudi labour law places the obligation on the employer/kafeel, but many employment contracts transfer this cost to the employee. Always check your contract before bringing family to Saudi Arabia.
Is the dependent levy the same as Iqama renewal? ▼
No — these are two completely different fees. Iqama renewal (SAR 650/year) renews the residency document. The dependent levy (SAR 400/month) is an ongoing monthly charge for having dependents on your visa. Both must be paid separately.
