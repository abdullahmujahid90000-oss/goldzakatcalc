Understanding Final Settlement in Saudi Arabia (2026 Complete Guide)

When an employment contract ends in Saudi Arabia — whether through resignation, termination, retirement, or contract expiry — employees are entitled to a comprehensive final settlement package under the Saudi Labor Law. This settlement ensures fair compensation for years of service, unused leave, and contractual obligations. Our free calculator helps employees accurately compute their total dues using official Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development (HRSD) guidelines for 2026.

📜 Key Components of Final Settlement

  • End of Service (EOS) Gratuity: Calculated based on years of service and reason for leaving. Use our EOS Calculator for precise gratuity amounts.
  • Vacation Encashment: Unused annual leave days paid at the daily basic rate: (Basic Monthly Salary ÷ 30) × Unused Days.
  • Unpaid Salary: Compensation for days worked in the final month that haven't been paid yet.
  • Notice Period Compensation/Deduction: If the employer fails to give notice, you receive full month salary compensation. If you fail to serve notice, it may be deducted.
  • Other Adjustments: Return ticket refund, documented bonuses, or legal deductions (loans, advances with consent).

⚖️ Legal Payment Deadline: Employers must pay the full final settlement within 10 days of termination (Labor Law Article 86). Delays entitle employees to claim additional compensation through HRSD.

💡 Final Settlement Rules Explained (2026)

EOS GratuityVaries — Based on years of service & exit reason
Vacation PayBasic ÷ 30 — Per unused day encashed
Notice PeriodFull Month — Compensation or deduction
Payment Deadline10 Days — From termination date (Article 86)

Key Labor Law Provisions:

  • Article 86: Employer must pay all dues within 10 days. Delays allow additional compensation claims.
  • Article 77: Unused annual leave encashed at basic salary rate. Usually capped at current + 1 previous year.
  • Article 75: Notice period compensation applies if either party fails to serve contractual notice.

Important: Arbitrary deductions are illegal. Employers may only deduct documented loans, court orders, or HRSD-approved damages.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Final Settlement

When must final settlement be paid?+

Within 10 days of termination per Article 86. Delays allow employees to claim additional compensation through HRSD.

How is vacation encashment calculated?+

(Basic Monthly Salary ÷ 30) × Number of unused annual leave days. Only basic salary is used, not allowances.

Can employer deduct from final settlement?+

Only for documented loans/advances with consent, court orders, or HRSD-approved damages. Arbitrary deductions are illegal.
